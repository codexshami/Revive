Diabetes Predictor

Diabetes occurs when blood glucose is too high. Insulin helps glucose enter cells.

Symptoms
  • Urinating often
  • Feeling very thirsty
  • Extreme fatigue
  • Blurry vision
🎗️

Breast Cancer Predictor

Cancer forms in breast cells, most common in women but men can also be affected.

Symptoms
  • Lump or thickening
  • Change in size/shape
  • Skin dimpling
  • Redness / pitting
❤️

Heart Disease Predictor

Coronary artery disease clogs arteries with plaque.

Symptoms
  • chest pain
  • breathlessness
  • palpitations
  • nausea
  • sweating
  • fatigue
🧫
Kidney Predictor

Chronic kidney disease gradually loses kidney function.

Symptoms
  • Nausea
  • Vomiting
  • Fatigue
  • Muscle cramps
🧪
Liver Predictor

Symptoms include swelling, bruising, jaundice, colour changes.

Malaria Predictor

Mosquito-borne disease causing fever, chills, headache.

Symptoms
  • Fever
  • Chills
  • Headache
  • Nausea
🫁
Pneumonia Predictor

Infection inflames air sacs, causing cough, fever, difficulty breathing.

Symptoms
  • Cough with mucus
  • Fever & chills
  • Shortness of breath
  • Rapid breathing
